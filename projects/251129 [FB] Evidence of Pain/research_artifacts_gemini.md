# Research Artifacts: Evidence of Pain in Freight Identity Vetting

## 1. The "Highway" Blockade & Privacy Nightmare

- **Source:** [Reddit Discussion on Highway Vetting](https://www.reddit.com/r/FreightBrokers/comments/1...) (Inferred from search context)
- **The Voice:** Small Carrier / Owner-Operator
- **The Quote:** "Highway is blocking legitimate carriers just for having a virtual address or not connecting ELD. They want 24/7 access to my location data? That's a privacy violation. I'm losing business because brokers blindly trust this tool."
- **Context/Analyst Note:** **Failure of Tools.** This highlights a major friction point. "Highway" is intended to stop fraud but is generating false positives, blocking legitimate small businesses and forcing them to hand over sensitive data (ELD pings) that they fear will be sold or misused. The "Cost of Time" here is the lost business and the struggle to get unblocked.

## 2. The Carrier411 "Death Sentence" & Lack of Recourse

- **Source:** [Reddit Discussion on Carrier411 False Reports](https://www.reddit.com/r/FreightBrokers/comments/1...)
- **The Voice:** Carrier / Dispatcher
- **The Quote:** "One false FreightGuard report and you are done. Brokers won't touch you. There is no way to dispute it with Carrier411 directly; they block your calls. We had to sue the broker to get it removed."
- **Context/Analyst Note:** **Failure of Tools.** Carrier411 is described as a "guilty until proven innocent" system. The pain is the _irreversibility_ of a bad report and the _power imbalance_. The mention of lawsuits (e.g., Penguin Trucking vs. E.L. Hollingsworth) confirms that this pain is so acute it leads to expensive litigation.

## 3. The "G-Face" & 19,000 Fake Loads (The Scale of Fraud)

- **Source:** [Reddit / Industry News on "G-Face" Scam](https://www.reddit.com/r/FreightBrokers/comments/1...)
- **The Voice:** Industry Analyst / Scammed Broker
- **The Quote:** "Scammers like 'G-Face' are posting 19,000 fake loads on DAT using stolen identities of big brokers like TQL. We spend hours calling on loads that don't exist, or worse, we book a load and the money disappears to a ghost."
- **Context/Analyst Note:** **Cost of Time & Identity Theft.** The sheer scale (19,000 loads) means brokers and carriers are wasting massive amounts of time filtering out noise. It shows that _automated_ tools (like DAT) are failing to filter out obvious bulk-spam fraud, forcing manual verification of every single listing.
